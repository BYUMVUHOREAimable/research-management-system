About

The Research Management System (RMS) is an integrated platform designed to streamline the process of managing research projects. It enables researchers to organize and track the progress of their work, collaborate with team members, and ensure that all data and tasks are aligned for successful outcomes.

Our system enhances collaboration, improves task management, and provides clear visibility into project statuses. With its user-friendly interface, researchers can easily add, edit, and track tasks, making the entire research process more efficient and organized. The RMS is committed to supporting academic excellence by facilitating seamless communication and project management.
